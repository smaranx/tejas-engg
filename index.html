About us

M/S. Tejas Engineering is a proprietary firm owned and managed by Mr. Deepak Ramchandar Nachankar, Our firm is carry out fabrication of heavy structures of Mild steel and erection of the same on site at various heights and elevation. We are specialized in critical erection jobs.
